How it Works

1 Enter Your Amount

Simply type the amount in UAE Dirhams (AED) you want to convert in the input field on the homepage. Our converter accepts decimal values for precise calculations.

2 Instant Conversion

As you type, our app automatically converts your AED amount to multiple currencies using real-time exchange rates. There's no need to click any buttons - it's all instant!

3 View All Currencies

See the converted amounts for all major currencies at once, displayed with their respective country flags. Our clean interface makes it easy to find the currency you're looking for.

4 Mobile-Friendly Design

Use the app on any device - it works perfectly on both desktop and mobile browsers. The responsive design ensures a great experience no matter your screen size.

5 Install for Offline Use

Make the most of our Progressive Web App (PWA) features:

  • Install the app on your device for quick access
  • Access basic features even without internet connection
  • Get the native app experience without taking up much storage space
